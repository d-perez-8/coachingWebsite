Who am I

I am a Career Counselor who develops strategies for young professionals

As a career coach with a master's degree in career counseling, I've dedicated over 30 years of my career to helping 18-25 year-old "emerging adults" at a world-renowned research university transition toward full adult independence.

In this university setting, I've hired, supervised, and coached hundreds of students, honing their marketable skills and equipping them for various careers both inside and outside of higher education.

In addition to my university experience, I've broadened my expertise by serving as an academic and career counselor at different community colleges. These roles exposed me to a broad spectrum of students, each confronting their own set of challenges on their path to adulthood. It enabled me to better understand and guide those exploring vocational career paths and those who needed additional academic preparation before pursuing a four-year degree post-high school.

My passion lies in assisting and guiding young adults as they navigate the often-challenging transition from adolescence to adulthood. I believe in the power of personalized coaching, skill-building exercises, and practical job search strategies to help my clients identify their unique strengths and explore potential career paths.

Recognizing that this can be an overwhelming and uncertain time for both young adults and their parents, I am committed to creating a supportive and nurturing environment throughout the coaching process. I pride myself on my ability to connect with my clients on a personal level, understand their unique needs, and help them navigate through their fears and insecurities in the world of work.

With a holistic approach to career coaching, I not only assist my clients in achieving their professional goals but also focus on developing their confidence, resilience, and life skills, so they can effectively move into the future. Whether it's exploring college and career options, preparing for job interviews, or navigating the complexities of the modern job market, I am here to support and guide them every step of the way.

Work with me, and together, we'll help you unlock your child’s potential, align their career choice with your interests and values, and help them step confidently into the future.
